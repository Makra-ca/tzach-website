A Home Away From Home

The Lubavitch Youth Organization (LYO) was established in 1955 under the direction of the Lubavitcher Rebbe to serve the Jewish community of the greater New York area.

Today, LYO coordinates over 200 Chabad Houses across NYC, Long Island, and Westchester, each serving as a warm, welcoming center for Jewish life, learning, and celebration.
