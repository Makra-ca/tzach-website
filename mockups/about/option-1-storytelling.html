About LYO

Seven decades of bringing the warmth of Judaism to every corner of the NYC Metro area.

"One should focus on tangible actions"
— The Rebbe, at the 1955 launch of LYO

The Lubavitch Youth Organization was established by the seventh Chabad-Lubavitch Rebbe, Rabbi Menachem Mendel Schneerson zy"a, to provide a wide range of services and educational programs for Jewish youth and adults of all ages and backgrounds.

Our History

The establishment of LYO was part of the Rebbe's broader vision to invigorate Jewish life globally through extensive outreach and social services, building on the foundations laid by his father-in-law, the sixth Rebbe, Rabbi Yosef Yitzchak Schneersohn.

He saw LYO as a means to involve the greater Chabad community in outreach activities, translating the core philosophy of Chabad—reaching every single Jew with unconditional love and acceptance—into action.
