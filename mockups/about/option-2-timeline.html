Our Story

Seven decades of connecting Jews in the NYC Metro area with their heritage and community.

1955

LYO Founded

The Rebbe established LYO with the vision to focus on "tangible actions" (peulas m'mashi'os) to reach every Jew.

1960s-70s

Expansion

LYO expanded throughout NYC, establishing Chabad Houses in Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens neighborhoods.

1980s-90s

Long Island & Westchester

The network grew to include Nassau, Suffolk, and Westchester counties, reaching Jewish families in the suburbs.

2000s

Campus Initiative

Major expansion to college campuses, now serving over 60 campuses across the NYC Metro area.

Today

200+ Shluchim, 170 Centers

The largest Jewish population center outside of Israel, part of a global network of 3,500+ Chabad institutions.
