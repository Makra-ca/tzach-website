Our Services

Chabad Houses offer comprehensive programs for Jewish life. Here's what you'll find at our locations across NYC Metro.

Youth Groups

Building Jewish identity from a young age through engaging programs like Tzivos Hashem for children, Hebrew Schools, and CTeen for teenagers.

Tzivos Hashem Hebrew School CTeen

Jewish Education

JLI courses and Torah classes for adults of all backgrounds. Explore the depth of Jewish wisdom through modern, engaging learning.

JLI Courses Torah Study One-on-One

Campus Outreach

Chabad on 60+ college campuses across the NYC Metro area. A home away from home for Jewish students with Shabbat dinners, holiday celebrations, and more.

60+ Campuses Shabbat Dinners Student Events

Community Events

Major public celebrations including the Columbus Circle Menorah lighting, Purim parades, and community gatherings that bring Jews together.

Menorah Lightings Purim Parties Public Events

Holiday Programs

Experience the joy of Jewish holidays with Shabbat dinners, High Holiday services, Passover Seders, Chanukah celebrations, and more.

Shabbat High Holidays Passover Chanukah

Lifecycle Events

Guidance and support for life's milestones including Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, weddings, and other celebrations.

Bar/Bat Mitzvah Weddings Celebrations
